Fish Tank Tips & Tricks

Maintaining Fish Tank pH

To lower pH, use natural driftwood or peat moss. To raise pH, add crushed coral to your filter.

Other Fish Tank Tips

Always test water pH before adding it to your tank. Consider reverse osmosis or deionized water to maintain the desired pH balance.
