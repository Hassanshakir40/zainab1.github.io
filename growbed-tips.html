Grow Bed Tips & Tricks

Maintaining Grow Bed pH

To improve soil pH in low-pH locations, mix finely ground limestone into the soil before planting or fertilize with calcium nitrate as recommended by soil tests.

Other Grow Bed Tips

Mix 2-4 inches of compost or well-rotted manure into the top 6-8 inches of soil to buffer pH fluctuations and provide nutrients.
