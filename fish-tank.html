Fish Tank Optimization

Optimum conditions for fish tank:

  • pH: 6.5-8.5 (Current: 7.8)
  • Dissolved O₂: 5-8ppm (Current: 6.5ppm)
  • Nutrients: 5-20mg/l (Current: 5.5mg/l)
  • Temperature: 75-80° F (Current: 79.5° F)
